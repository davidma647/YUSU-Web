Custom & OEM

Our custom packaging process:

  • Step 1: Concept (Day 1-3): Share your idea, sketch, or reference images.
  • Step 2: Design (Day 4-10): Our R&D team creates 3D renderings for your approval.
  • Step 3: Mold Development (Day 11-35): We manufacture your exclusive mold in-house.
  • Step 4: Sample (Day 36-45): Receive physical samples for testing.
  • Step 5: Production (Day 46-65): Mass production begins after sample approval.

Please provide:
  • Your brand logo (AI or PDF format, vector preferred)
  • Color specifications (Pantone codes if available)
  • Artwork files for printing
  • Any regulatory requirements for your market
We assist with design if you don't have artwork ready.

Customization MOQ

Quality & Certifications

YUSU Packaging is recognized as one of Top 100 China Beauty Packaging Enterprises (2025). We follow ISO 9001 Quality Management Standards, GMP, and environmental compliance standards. All products pass internal QC before shipment.

Our quality control process includes:
  1. Raw Material Inspection: Check all incoming materials
  2. In-Process QC: Monitor production at key stages
  3. Final Inspection: 100% visual check + AQL sampling
  4. Pre-Shipment Audit: Third-party inspection available

Yes. We welcome third-party inspections from SGS, Bureau Veritas, Intertek, or your own QC team. Please inform us at least 3 days before the inspection date.

Yes. All our packaging materials are FDA-compliant for cosmetic contact, free from harmful substances (lead, cadmium, mercury), and tested for compatibility with common cosmetic formulas. MSDS available upon request.
