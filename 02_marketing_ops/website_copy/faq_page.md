#### Q2: What materials do you use for foundation bottles?
**A**: We offer glass, PETG, and acrylic options. Glass bottles provide premium weight and feel. PETG is lightweight and shatter-resistant. Our team can recommend the best material for your product formula and brand positioning.

**Target Keyword**: cosmetic bottle materials comparison

- Better protection for sensitive formulas
- Recyclable and eco-friendly image
- Superior weight that signals quality to consumers

**Target Keyword**: glass foundation bottle benefits

### Category 2: Ordering & MOQ

#### Q5: What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
**A**: Our MOQ starts at **10,000 pieces** for stock designs. This applies to most foundation bottles, lipstick tubes, and air cushion compacts. For custom mold development, MOQ depends on design complexity. [Contact us](/contact) for a quote.

**Target Keyword**: cosmetic packaging MOQ

---

#### Q6: How do I place an order from outside China?
**A**: Ordering is simple:
1. **Inquiry**: Send your requirements via our [Contact Form](/contact)
2. **Quote**: Receive pricing within 24-48 hours
3. **Sample**: Approve samples before production
4. **Order**: Confirm order and payment terms
5. **Production**: Track progress with your dedicated account manager
6. **Delivery**: Receive goods at your location

We work with buyers from Southeast Asia, Europe, and North America daily.

**Target Keyword**: how to order cosmetic bottles from china

All payments are in USD.

**A**: Standard production time:
- **Stock designs**: 15-20 days

We provide production updates every 5 days. Rush orders available for additional fee.

**Target Keyword**: cosmetic bottle production time

### Category 4: Custom & OEM

#### Q13: What is your custom packaging process?
**A**: Our custom packaging process:

**Step 1: Concept (Day 1-3)**  
Share your idea, sketch, or reference images.

**Step 2: Design (Day 4-10)**  
Our R&D team creates 3D renderings for your approval.

**Step 3: Mold Development (Day 11-35)**  
We manufacture your exclusive mold in-house.

**Step 4: Sample (Day 36-45)**  
Receive physical samples for testing.

**Step 5: Production (Day 46-65)**  
Mass production begins after sample approval.

**Total Timeline**: 45-60 days for new custom designs.

**Target Keyword**: custom cosmetic packaging process

---

#### Q14: What do I need to provide for OEM orders?
**A**: For OEM orders, please provide:
- Your brand logo (AI or PDF format, vector preferred)
- Color specifications (Pantone codes if available)
- Artwork files for printing
- Any regulatory requirements for your market

We assist with design if you don't have artwork ready.

**Target Keyword**: OEM cosmetic packaging requirements

---

#### Q15: What is the MOQ for private label packaging?
**A**: Private label MOQ depends on customization level:
| Customization | MOQ |
|---------------|-----|
| Logo printing on stock design | 10,000 pcs |
| Custom color on stock design | 20,000 pcs |
| New mold (exclusive design) | 30,000 pcs |

Lower MOQ may be possible for long-term partnerships.

**Target Keyword**: private label packaging MOQ

---

#### Q16: Can you print my logo on the packaging?
**A**: Yes. We offer multiple printing options:
- **Silk Screen Printing**: Best for solid colors, durable
- **Hot Stamping**: Metallic gold/silver finishes
- **UV Printing**: Full-color, photographic images
- **Embossing/Debossing**: Raised or recessed textures

Share your logo and we'll recommend the best method for your design.

**Target Keyword**: cosmetic packaging logo printing

---

### Category 5: Quality & Certifications

#### Q17: What quality certifications do you have?
**A**: YUSU Packaging is recognized as one of **Top 100 China Beauty Packaging Enterprises (2025)**. We follow:
- ISO 9001 Quality Management Standards
- Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP)
- Environmental compliance standards

All products pass internal QC before shipment.

---

#### Q18: How do you ensure product quality?
**A**: Our quality control process includes:
1. **Raw Material Inspection**: Check all incoming materials
2. **In-Process QC**: Monitor production at key stages
3. **Final Inspection**: 100% visual check + AQL sampling
4. **Pre-Shipment Audit**: Third-party inspection available

Defect rate is kept below 1%.

---

#### Q19: Can I arrange third-party inspection?
**A**: Yes. We welcome third-party inspections from:
- SGS
- Bureau Veritas
- Intertek
- Your own QC team

Please inform us at least 3 days before the inspection date.

---

#### Q20: Are your products safe for cosmetic use?
**A**: Yes. All our packaging materials are:
- FDA-compliant for cosmetic contact
- Free from harmful substances (lead, cadmium, mercury)
- Tested for compatibility with common cosmetic formulas

Material safety data sheets (MSDS) available upon request.
