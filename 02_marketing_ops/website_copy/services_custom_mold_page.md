## FAQ Accordion

**H2**: Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1**: What files do you need for design-based customization?  
**A1**: We accept 2D design files (AI, PDF, CDR), 3D models (STEP, IGES, STL), or even hand-drawn sketches. Our R&D team reviews and optimizes for production.

**Q2**: How long does custom mold development take?  
**A2**: Standard mold development takes 15-25 days. Complex designs may require 30-45 days. We provide progress updates at each milestone.

**Q3**: What is the MOQ for private mold products?  
**A3**: MOQ varies by product complexity. Typically 3,000-5,000 units for simple designs, 5,000-10,000 for complex structures. Contact us for a quote.

**Q4**: Can you replicate a competitor's design?  
**A4**: We conduct IP due diligence before any replication project. If a design is protected, we suggest modifications or original alternatives that capture your desired aesthetic.

**Q5**: Is my custom design protected?  
**A5**: Yes. All private mold designs are exclusive to your brand. We offer NDA signing and can assist with IP registration if needed.
